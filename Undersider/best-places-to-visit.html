Best Places to Visit in Michigan

1. Leelanau Peninsula

The Leelanau Peninsula is a stunning and beautiful piece of Michigan that boasts endless amounts of natural beauty and recreational activities. This beautiful region is a perfect location to explore for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and anyone looking for a fun and exciting trip.

2. Keweenaw Peninsula

Concentrated beauty and history. The Keweenaw Peninsula is a scenic and historical treasure. The beauty of the north woods and Lake Superior shoreline is reason enough to make the Keweenaw country a destination attraction, and the copper mining history of the Houghton-Hancock-Calumet area is a major bonus.

3. Tahquamenon Falls State Park

Experience one of the United States' most breathtaking waterfalls! Set within a pristine wilderness, the Upper Tahquamenon Falls is over 200 feet wide and 48 feet high. This is definitely one of the prettiest places I have ever seen.
