The Best Beaches in Michigan

1. Petoskey Beach

Glittering blue water and soft, warm sand make the beaches along Little Traverse Bay and Michigan's inland lakes reason alone to vacation here. Take a dip, catch some rays, build a sandcastle. This is the best beach I have ever been to in my entire life, and I'm 99% sure it will be yours as well.

2. Ludington State Park

Ludington State Park, situated between the 5,000-acre Hamlin Lake and 7 miles of sandy Lake Michigan shoreline, features scenic sand dunes, three campgrounds, the iconic Big Sable Point Lighthouse, 21 miles of marked trails and more. A 1-mile stretch of the Big Sable River runs through the park and provides excellent opportunities for fishing, paddling and tubing. This is an absolutely stunning place to visit with your family if you're wanting to get an escape from reality.

3. Tawas Point State Park

The park is home to a modern campground and overnight lodging facilities, popular for their location on Tawas Bay, where the water is shallow and warm for swimming. The park’s day-use area features a popular swimming beach, a picnic area and nature trail along the picturesque Lake Huron coast.
